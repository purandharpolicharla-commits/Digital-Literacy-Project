DESCRIPTION:
I created my digital literacy infographic using Canva because it is simple and easy to design visuals.
In my infographic, I explained what digital literacy means and why it is important for students. 
I included topics like useful digital tools, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and email etiquette.
I used short points and simple design so that anyone can understand it quickly.
The infographic shows how students can use tools like Google Docs, Gmail, and GitHub in their daily academic work.
It also explains how to stay safe online by using strong passwords and avoiding unknown links.
I also added information about maintaining a professional profile and writing proper emails.
While making this, I found it a bit difficult to adjust all the content into one page without making it look messy. 
But at the same time, it was interesting to choose colors, icons, and arrange everything neatly. 
Overall, this task helped me improve both my creativity and my understanding of digital literacy.
